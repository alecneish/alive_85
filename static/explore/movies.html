Movies

The decade that defined blockbusters

From sci-fi epics to teen comedies, the 80s gave us films that shaped every genre. Here are the ones that still hit.

🗼
1982 Sci-Fi

Blade Runner

Ridley Scott's neon-drenched vision of 2019 LA. Replicants, rain, and Rutger Hauer's tears in the rain monologue.

🎓
1985 Classic

The Breakfast Club

Five high schoolers. One Saturday detention. John Hughes defined a generation with this one.

⚡
1985 Essential

Back to the Future

Marty McFly, a DeLorean, and 1.21 gigawatts. The most rewatchable film of the decade.

👻
1984

Ghostbusters

Who you gonna call? Bill Murray, Dan Aykroyd, and the Stay Puft Marshmallow Man.

🛸
1982

E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial

Spielberg made the whole world cry over a wrinkly alien with a glowing finger.

💥
1988 Action

Die Hard

Bruce Willis in a tank top vs. Alan Rickman in a suit. Yes, it's a Christmas movie.

🎶
1986

Ferris Bueller's Day Off

Life moves pretty fast. If you don't stop and look around once in a while, you could miss it.

🤖
1984

The Terminator

He said he'd be back. James Cameron launched a franchise and made Schwarzenegger a legend.
