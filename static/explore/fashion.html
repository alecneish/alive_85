Fashion

Neon, denim, and zero subtlety

80s fashion was loud, layered, and unapologetically bold. From power suits to leg warmers, here's what defined the look of the decade.

Power Suits

Shoulder pads wide enough to land a plane on. The power suit said 'I'm in charge' before you opened your mouth.

1986
