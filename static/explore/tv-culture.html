TV & Culture

The shows, ads, and moments we lived

Before streaming, before DVR — you watched it live or you missed it. The 80s gave us sitcoms, Saturday morning cartoons, and cultural moments that united the entire country.

🌴
1984 Iconic

Miami Vice

Pastel suits, no socks, Phil Collins on the soundtrack. Don Johnson made crime-fighting look like a fashion show.

📺
1984

The Cosby Show

The #1 show in America for five straight years. Changed the sitcom landscape.
