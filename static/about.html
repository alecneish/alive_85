Born 20 Years
Too Late

Built by a college kid who was born 20 years too late. Growing up hearing stories about the 80s — the music, the movies, the way people actually lived — he wanted to bring the magic of the 80s back to the future (no pun intended).

alive85 is part store, part time machine. Through collector's items, curated 80s movies, music, and daily trivia, we're here to give you a trip down memory lane — or an introduction to the decade you missed.

Everything we sell is designed to help you rewind, unplug, and be present.

Put the phone down. Go make some memories.
